About Me

Hi there, my name is Niveditha Madegowda.

I am a passionate engineer with a strong inclination toward software development and artificial intelligence, specializing in areas such as deep learning, computer vision, and data-driven solutions. My academic journey, bolstered by a Master's in Electrical Engineering at USC, has equipped me with the technical depth and problem-solving skills needed to design intelligent, user-focused systems. My interests in machine learning and computer vision drive my enthusiasm for building impactful technologies that address real-world challenges while embracing the latest advancements in AI and software engineering.

My professional experience spans roles at renowned organizations and includes a notable internship at ISRO, where I worked on detecting plant diseases using machine learing and image processing, a project that also led to an IEEE publication. At Tata Elxsi, I honed my software engineering skills, optimizing game templates and integrating AI into edutainment games, earning multiple awards for my contributions. My tenure at Element Technologies further solidified my expertise in AI and software development, where I implemented tools that significantly improved operational efficiency for various clients. As an AI Lead at WorkUp, I enhanced job recommendation systems and explored the potential of AI agents to streamline operations. These experiences have sharpened my ability to lead, collaborate, and deliver impactful solutions that align with industry needs.
